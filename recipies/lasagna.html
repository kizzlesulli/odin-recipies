Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is mexican, and traditional. Generations love it.

Slap some pasta. Slap some meat. Slap some cheese. Tomato.

Ingredients

  • Frilly pasta
  • Chunky meat
  • Flaky cheese
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta
  2. Butcher the meat
  3. Burn the cheese
  4. Tomato
