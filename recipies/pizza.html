Pizza

Description

It's flat. It's simple. It's tasty.

Everyone likes pizza. For breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

Ingredients

  • Flat dough
  • Round pepperoni
  • Mozza Cheese
  • Onions
  • Mushrooms
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Dough down
  2. Splat the tomato
  3. Frisbie the pepperoni
  4. Salt bae the cheese
  5. Air dice the mushrooms and onions
  6. Salt bae the cheese
