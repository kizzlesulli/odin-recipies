Burrito

Description

Food tubes are the best delivery system.

Load the tube up with whatever you like and torpedo it down your throat.

Ingredients

  • Bread tube
  • Rice filler
  • Green peppers
  • Onions
  • Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Flat your bread
  2. Mix all your filler
  3. Slap it all together
  4. Roll it up
